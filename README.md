# Stellar Space

Stellar Space

Stellar Space is a modern study and productivity platform built to help students, learners, and professionals stay consistent, motivated, and connected.
It provides a shared digital space where people from around the world can study together, build habits, and work toward their goals as a community.

🚀 Overview

Stellar Space transforms the solo study experience into something collaborative and energizing.
With global focus rooms, real-time interactions, and an encouraging community, the platform supports users in staying accountable and productive — anytime, anywhere.

✨ Features
🌎 Global Study Rooms

Join live study spaces with users from different countries and time zones. Stay motivated by working alongside others who share similar goals.

⏳ Focus Timers & Productivity Tools

Use built-in timers, routines, and productivity aids to help maintain consistency and track progress during study sessions.

💬 Community Interaction

Connect with other students through subtle, non-distracting features that make studying feel supportive and collaborative.

👥 Real People, Real Growth

Study with a community that values discipline and progress. Build habits, celebrate small wins, and stay accountable.

🧘 Distraction-Free Environment

A clean and calm interface designed to help you focus on what matters most — your work, your goals, and your growth.

📢 Early Access Community

Join the Stellar Space community early to receive updates, participate in events, and help shape the future of the platform.
